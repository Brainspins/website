Anna Musikhina is a media artist, researcher, and performer. Her artistic practice explores the relationship between individuals, collectives, and technology through the framework of Digital Wisdom. In this perspective, technology takes centre stage rather than the human. She previously worked at an IT company specialising in biometric technologies such as facial and voice recognition. This experience gave her a deep understanding of the technical, social, and ethical dimensions of data usage. Influenced by this background, she sees the main goal of Digital Wisdom as fostering awareness of the consequences of our interactions within digital environments, including digital footprints and algorithmic structures. In her practice, Anna uses AI, technology, and algorithms both as creative tools and as critical subjects. This approach allows her to shift the perspective from technological invisibility to a deeper examination of the hidden dynamics of algorithmic control, digital ecosystems, and their impact on human interaction.
